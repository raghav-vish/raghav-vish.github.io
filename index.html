About Me

Hello everyone!
My name is Raghav Vishwanath. I'm currently a student studying Electronics and Computer Engineering at VIT Chennai.

Coding, Cubing and Photography are some of my interests. I think I can say I'm decently good at them. More on these later.

Quizzing is also an interest of mine. I have participated in numerous intra-school and inter-school quizzes. The proudest moment in my quzzing career was when I came second in a state-level quiz. I haven't participated in too many quizzes recently but I hope to get back to it soon.

Coding

Programming is a huge interest of mine.
Some of the languages I know, with varied levels of proficiency in each, are C, C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MATLAB and R.

I recently did an internship with IIIT Hyderabad. Most of my work there was related to porting their experiments from Java to JavaScript/HTML.

Some years ago, I qualified for, and participated in a national level coding competition organised by the Computer Society of India.

I've also helped organise workshops with VIThink, an AI research group on campus, to teach Python to our juniors.

Cubing

Cubing is one of my newer interests. I didn't know how to solve a Rubik's cube until about two and a half years ago, when a random Youtube video about the cube popped up in my Youtube recommendations.

I currently average under 14 seconds on the 3x3 cube, and under 4 seconds on the 2x2. So far, I have only attended two competitions, and hope to attend more in the future. Based on the results of my last competition, I currently rank among the top 500 in the country on 2x2 and 3x3.
