About Me

Hello everyone!
My name is Raghav Vishwanath. I'm currently a student studying Electronics and Computer Engineering at VIT Chennai.

Programming is a huge interest of mine. Some of the languages I know, with varied levels of proficiency in each, are C, C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MATLAB and R. I recently did an internship with IIIT Hyderabad. Most of my work there was related to porting their experiments from Java to JavaScript/HTML.

Another huge interest of mine is cubing. Cubing has been a hobby of mine for a little over a year now, and with practice I've gotten decently good. I average around 17-18 seconds per solve. Nowhere near record speeds, but definitely better than average.

I'm also a huge geek. I'm a huge fan of the Harry Potter books and movies. I'm also a huge fan of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. This is not something I should probably brag about, but I can probably recite all the dialogues from the Harry Potter movies, Men in Black movies, and most of the movies from the MCU verbatim.
